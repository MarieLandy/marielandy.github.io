ABOUT

Marie Landy is an emerging production manager based in Melbourne/Naarm. Marie holds a Master of Business Management from EDHEC Business School, Paris, France. Marie has performed a variety of roles for high-profile companies in the fashion and entertainment industries, as well as for community theatre.
