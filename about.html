About

Marie Landy is an emerging international production manager.

Nurtured by a passion for storytelling through crafts and performance, Marie uses her Master’s in Business Management to contribute to design, artistic, and performance projects, all while maintaining a commitment to creative excellence and operational efficiency.

Marie’s career began in France, where she collaborated with national cultural institutions, the prestigious fashion house Hermès, and Walt Disney Imagineering. Her diverse experiences across these influential sectors honed her skills in managing multifaceted projects, coordinating cross-functional teams, and developing a deep understanding of various artistic disciplines, including technical, design, and logistical elements.

Recently, Marie has embraced a new chapter by relocating from Paris to Melbourne. This move marks an exciting transition as she integrates into the vibrant theatre community, where she has already made notable contributions through collaborations with Theatrical, AG Theatre, CGTC, and Reaction Theatre for La Mama's Mother Tongue Festival.

Marie is eager to engage in collaborations across diverse artistic fields and is driven to undertake both local and international projects.
